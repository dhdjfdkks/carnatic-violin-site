About Carnatic Violin

Carnatic music, the classical tradition of South India, is known for its melodic depth and rhythmic intricacy. The violin, introduced during the colonial period, found a unique space in this tradition. Adapted for seated playing and capable of expressing gamakas (ornamentations), it became one of the most beloved instruments in Carnatic concerts.
